Huayi Li

pronunciation similar to [Howie Lee]

 

 

 

I joined Google (Mountain View, California) in June 2016. Before that I was pursuing my Ph.D. degree in Department of Computer Science, University of Illinois at Chicago supervised by Prof. Bing Liu. I worked in the Social Media and Data Mining (SMDM) lab focusing on Spam Detection, Sentiment Analysis and Social Network Analysis.
